To use ZoomWatch, you need to be on a Zoom meeting page.

1. Go to zoom.us and join a meeting
2. Once in the meeting, click the ZoomWatch extension icon in your browser toolbar
3. The side panel will open, allowing you to monitor participants and manage camera status

Note: ZoomWatch only works on Zoom meeting pages. Please navigate to a Zoom meeting to continue.
